The Problem

YouTube is engineered to keep you scrolling — not to help you find what you actually want.

Cluttered feeds, aggressive autoplay, and algorithmic rabbit holes make intentional viewing nearly impossible. Users lose hours to content they never chose.

The Solution

A focused YouTube video app that puts the viewer back in control.

Clean playback, intentional discovery, and personal curation — so users watch what they came to watch, save what matters, and leave when they're done.
