# Product Overview — YouTube Video App

## Product
A YouTube video application that lets users discover, browse, watch, and curate YouTube video content through a clean, focused interface — cutting through the noise of the default YouTube experience.

## Status
Early definition — core product direction to be refined through onboarding. App name and specific feature set TBD.

## Users / Audience
- **Primary**: Video enthusiasts and casual YouTube viewers seeking a better, less cluttered browsing and discovery experience
- **Secondary**: Content creators and researchers who need to track, organize, and revisit YouTube content efficiently

## Positioning
A streamlined YouTube video experience — smarter discovery, cleaner playback, and intentional organization. Built for people who want to watch on their own terms, not the algorithm's.

## Brand & Tone
- Modern, content-first, distraction-free
- Tone: calm, focused, and empowering — puts the user in control

## Core Scope (MVP)
- Search and browse YouTube videos within the app
- Watch YouTube videos in a clean, ad-reduced playback experience
- Save and organize favorite videos and playlists
- Discover trending or recommended content by category or interest

## Strategic Principles
- Video is the core experience — every UI decision serves the content, not the platform
- Speed and simplicity over feature sprawl
- User intent over algorithmic hijacking
- Minimal friction from search to play
